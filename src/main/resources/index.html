About BlueVision

BlueVision is an educational tool used to visualize different processes within a blockchain network. BlueVision is built on top of BlueChain, a distrubuted, decentralized blockchain network and research framework implemeneted in Java.

BlueVision contains two major components: a live view of the BlueChain network, and an interactive step-through view of the processes in the BlueChain network.

Upon pressing "Get Started!", you will be prompted to configure components within the BlueChain Network. You will decide the number of nodes in the network and the minimum amount of transactions the nodes need to create a block.

After these configurations are decided, you will launch the BlueChain network, and the network will begin to create transactions and add blocks onto the chain. You can then decide to watch a live view of the network as it runs or to walkthrough a visualization of the network at a specific sequence of blocks.
